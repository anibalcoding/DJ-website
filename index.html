About Me

Sounds that tells my story

I’m DJ Shutitdown, a bilingual DJ based in Dallas, TX, with over 15 years of experience creating unforgettable moments for quinceañeras, weddings, clubs, and private events. Music isn’t just what I play, it’s how I connect. My passion, energy, and deep ties to the community have helped me become a trusted name across DFW.

What I Do

I deliver a complete, high-quality DJ experience tailored to each event. From smooth live mixes to keeping the dance floor packed all night, I bring the perfect blend of professionalism and party atmosphere. Every event is crafted to match your vibe, culture, and vision, ensuring your big day feels authentically you.

What I Specialize In

  • Bilingual events (English & Spanish)
  • High-energy dance floors that stay alive all night
  • Regional Mexican, Hip Hop, Latin Trap, Corridos, Reggaeton & more

Whether it's a once in a lifetime celebration or a club night that needs a high energy, I bring the sound, style, and experience to make it unforgettable.
